Welcome to Gyankunja

Where Every Child's Potential is Nurtured

Quality Education in a Caring Environment

At Gyankunja English School, we believe in providing holistic education that develops not just academic excellence but also character, leadership, and social responsibility. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities create an ideal learning environment for students from pre-primary to secondary level.

We follow an enhanced curriculum that combines the best of Nepali values with global educational practices, preparing our students to excel in both national and international arenas.
