Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

What are the school timings?

School runs from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Sunday through Friday. The office is open from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions page or visit the school office to collect an application form.

Is there transportation facility?

Yes, we have school buses covering major routes in Dang. Contact the admin office for route details.

How can I contact a teacher?

You can schedule a meeting through the school office or email them directly using our staff directory.

What is the fee payment process?

Fees can be paid monthly at the school office or via bank transfer. Details are provided upon admission.

Do you have hostel facilities?

Yes, we have separate hostels for boys and girls with full-time wardens and healthy meals.
