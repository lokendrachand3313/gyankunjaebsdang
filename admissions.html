Documents Required

For New Admission

  • Birth Certificate (Original + 2 copies)
  • Recent passport size photos (4 copies)
  • Parents' citizenship copies
  • Previous school leaving certificate (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (for grades above 1)
  • Vaccination card (for pre-primary)

Admission Requirements by Grade

  • Pre-Primary: Age 3+ (Nursery), 4+ (LKG), 5+ (UKG)
  • Grade 1: Should have completed UKG
  • Grades 2-8: Previous grade pass certificate
  • Grades 9-10: SEE preparation with good foundation
