Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, promotes moral values, and develops responsible citizens who can contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence in Dang, producing well-rounded individuals who excel academically and possess strong character and leadership skills.

Our Journey Since 1985

Gyankunja English School was established in 1985 with just 50 students and 5 teachers. Over the past four decades, we have grown into one of Dang's most respected educational institutions, serving over 1,500 students from pre-primary to secondary level.

Our commitment to quality education, experienced faculty, and modern facilities has made us the first choice for parents seeking the best education for their children in Dang district.
